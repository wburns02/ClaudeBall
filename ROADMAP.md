# Claude Ball — Master Roadmap

## Phase 1: Simulation Realism (Critical)
1. **Pitcher fatigue auto-substitution** — AI pulls tired starters (100+ pitches, 70%+ fatigue), selects from bullpen
2. **Bullpen warmup system** — Relievers need 2-3 minutes to warm up before entering
3. **Pinch hitting AI** — CPU pinch-hits in late innings with better matchups
4. **Defensive substitutions** — Late-inning defensive replacements
5. **Double switches** — NL-style double switches when changing pitchers
6. **Platoon matchups** — AI considers L/R matchups for batting order
7. **Pitch count management** — Starters managed differently based on game score/situation
8. **Fatigue carryover** — Pitchers who pitched yesterday aren't available today
9. **Closer usage** — AI saves closer for 9th inning save situations only
10. **Setup man role** — 8th inning specialist usage
11. **Pinch runner AI** — Speed upgrades in late-game stolen base situations
12. **Intentional walk AI** — Walk the big bat to set up DP, pitch to weaker hitter
13. **Sacrifice bunt situations** — AI bunts with pitcher batting, late-game tying run at 2nd

## Phase 2: Player Customization (Full Editor)
14. **Player editor page** — Edit ANY player's ratings (1-100 sliders for all attributes)
15. **Create custom player** — Full player creator for any position (not just career mode)
16. **Team editor** — Change team name, city, colors, abbreviation
17. **Roster editor** — Move players between teams, release, sign
18. **Uniform customization** — Primary/secondary colors, pinstripe toggle
19. **Rating import/export** — JSON import/export of custom rosters
20. **Draft class editor** — Customize draft prospects before the draft
21. **Free agent pool editor** — Add/remove free agents, set ratings
22. **Custom league creator** — Create leagues with custom team count, divisions

## Phase 3: Gameplay Polish
23. **Close-up at-bat camera** — Zoomed view of pitcher/batter during pitch delivery
24. **Smooth sprite transitions** — Interpolate between sprite frames for buttery animation
25. **Ball visibility throughout play** — Ball always visible from pitcher to plate to field
26. **Camera pan to follow ball** — Camera smoothly tracks ball on hits
27. **Fielder movement to ball** — Fielders run to ball landing spot, not teleport
28. **Runner base-to-base movement** — Smooth running animation along basepaths
29. **Post-play camera reset** — Smooth zoom back to default after play ends
30. **Pitch type visualization** — Show pitch type name and speed briefly
31. **Strike zone box** — Semi-transparent overlay during at-bats
32. **Bat trail effect** — Motion blur on bat during swing
33. **Speed lines on fastball** — Visual effect showing pitch speed

## Phase 4: Season Simulation Depth
34. **Starting rotation management** — 5-man rotation auto-cycles through season
35. **Day-to-day injuries** — Players miss 1-15 days with minor injuries
36. **DL stints** — 15-day and 60-day disabled list
37. **September callups** — Expanded rosters in September
38. **Playoff roster selection** — 26-man playoff roster from 40-man
39. **Trade deadline** — No trades after July 31st
40. **Waiver wire** — Claim released players
41. **Rule 5 draft** — Protect/expose players
42. **Arbitration** — Salary arbitration for eligible players
43. **Contract extensions** — Re-sign players before free agency
44. **Minor league system** — AAA/AA/A affiliates with real rosters
45. **Player development** — Minor league performance affects ratings
46. **Scouting system** — Scout amateur players, uncertainty in ratings
